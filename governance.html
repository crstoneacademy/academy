Governance

The academy is being structured with accountability from the beginning. The goal is to avoid unhealthy concentration of power while maintaining clear leadership.

Board responsibilities

  • Protect the mission of the school
  • Provide financial oversight
  • Approve major policies
  • Hear escalated grievances when necessary

Leadership responsibilities

  • Manage day-to-day school operations
  • Lead academic and student support matters
  • Communicate with parents and staff
  • Implement board-approved policies

Safeguards

  • Board oversight without classroom micromanagement
  • Defined grievance path for parents and staff
  • No staff member disciplines or adjudicates their own child’s case
  • Major actions documented and reviewable
