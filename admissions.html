Admissions

We are building our founding class carefully and prayerfully. Because class size is intentionally small, admissions are based on fit, readiness, and available space.

Admissions process

  1. Initial inquiry
  2. Family conversation or tour
  3. Application submission
  4. Student records review
  5. Student meeting or readiness assessment
  6. Enrollment decision

What we consider

  • Alignment with the school’s mission and expectations
  • Academic readiness for the classroom model
  • Behavior and support needs
  • Available capacity in the founding class
Founding class target: 5–8 students across grades 2 and 3.
