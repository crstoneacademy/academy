Tuition

Cornerstone Christian Academy is committed to keeping tuition as accessible as possible while still supporting classroom space, curriculum, supplies, and basic school operations.

Tuition rate

Monthly tuition$750
Annual tuition$7,500 per student
School year10-month payment model

Tuition philosophy

Many nearby private-school options cost well over this level. Our aim is to provide a strong Christian education with lower overhead and a simpler structure, while recognizing that rent, insurance, curriculum, and operational costs are real and unavoidable.
