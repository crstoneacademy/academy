What makes Cornerstone different

We are building a school framework that is intentionally small, family-centered, and governed with accountability. The goal is a school culture that is warm, respectful, and clear—not chaotic, not heavy-handed.

Small-class instruction

Students are known, guided, and taught with intentionality in a multi-grade classroom model.

Structured and loving

Order matters, but dignity matters too. Policies are written to protect students, parents, and staff from confusion or misuse of authority.

Biblical foundation

Instruction is rooted in Christian belief, moral formation, and academic strength.

Quick facts
