Staff Ethics

Staff are expected to model Christian character, professional conduct, and healthy boundaries.

  • Students must be treated with dignity and fairness.
  • Parents must be treated with respect even during disagreement.
  • Favoritism, intimidation, and humiliating discipline are prohibited.
  • Concerns about employee treatment may be raised through the same grievance path published by the school.
