Conflict Resolution

Cornerstone Christian Academy seeks to address conflict directly, respectfully, and fairly.

Resolution path

  1. Speak directly with the person closest to the issue.
  2. If unresolved, request leadership review.
  3. If still unresolved, seek board review.

Staff-child cases

When a concern involves the child of a staff member, that staff member is removed from the decision process. Another leader or the board handles the matter to avoid conflicts of interest.
