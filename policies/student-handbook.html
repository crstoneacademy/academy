Student Handbook

Students are expected to learn, grow, and relate to others in a way that reflects Christian character.

Student expectations

  • Show respect to teachers, classmates, and school property
  • Participate honestly and responsibly in classwork
  • Practice self-control and kindness
  • Use words and actions that build up rather than tear down

Discipline philosophy

Discipline is intended to teach responsibility and restore relationships. Humiliating, degrading, or excessive discipline is not allowed.
