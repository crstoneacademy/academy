Parent Handbook

This handbook is written to support a respectful partnership between families and the school.

Parent partnership

Parents are the primary educators of their children. The school exists to support the Christian home through clear communication, mutual respect, and a shared commitment to student growth.

Communication

Parents should address routine academic or classroom concerns with the teacher first. If unresolved, concerns may be elevated to school leadership and then to board review when appropriate.

Attendance

Regular attendance matters. Parents should notify the school when a child is absent and work with the teacher to complete missed work when needed.
