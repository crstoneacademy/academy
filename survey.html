Family Interest Survey

We are currently gathering input from families interested in a small Christian school environment. Your feedback will help shape the early direction of Cornerstone Christian Academy.

Survey responses should be routed to: cstoneacademy@zohomail.com

Create the Google Form

Suggested Survey Questions

  1. Parent/guardian name
  2. Email address
  3. City or area where you live
  4. How many children are you considering for Christian school?
  5. What grade level(s) are you looking for?
  6. What kind of school setting are you most interested in?
  7. How important is a distinctly Christian worldview in your child's education?
  8. What is your ideal class size?
  9. How many days per week would best fit your family?
  10. What kind of daily schedule would be most helpful to your family?
  11. Which academic priorities matter most to you?
  12. What school qualities matter most when choosing a school?
  13. Would you be interested in a small school that emphasizes family partnership?
  14. Would you be open to volunteer opportunities or special events involvement?
  15. How interested would you be in receiving updates as Cornerstone Christian Academy develops?
  16. What do you feel is missing from current school options in your area?
