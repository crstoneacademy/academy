About Cornerstone Christian Academy

Cornerstone Christian Academy exists to assist Christian families by providing a Christ-centered, academically strong education in a small and nurturing environment where students grow spiritually, intellectually, and morally.

Mission

To partner with families in cultivating students who love God, pursue truth, develop wisdom, demonstrate character, and serve others with humility.

Vision

To develop students who think critically, live biblically, serve joyfully, and build their lives on Christ the Cornerstone.

Core commitments

  • Parents remain the primary educators of their children.
  • The school exists to support—not replace—the Christian home and church.
  • Authority must be servant-based, accountable, and respectful.
  • Strong academics and character formation belong together.

Statement of faith summary

Cornerstone Christian Academy affirms the historic Christian faith: the authority of Scripture, the Trinity, the lordship of Jesus Christ, salvation by grace through faith, and the dignity of every person as an image-bearer of God. A fuller statement can be published on a dedicated governance page as the school finalizes its founding documents.
