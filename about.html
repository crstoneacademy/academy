About Cornerstone Christian Academy

Cornerstone Christian Academy seeks to provide Christ-centered education in a small classroom environment where students are known, mentored, and encouraged to grow in wisdom and faith.

Our goal is to partner with families to develop students who love God, pursue truth, and demonstrate strong character.

Our Commitments

Truth
We believe education should be rooted in what is true, good, and lasting.
Character
We want students to grow in humility, discipline, kindness, and responsibility.
Wisdom
We value strong academic foundations and thoughtful learning.
Partnership
We believe parents are essential partners in a child’s education.
